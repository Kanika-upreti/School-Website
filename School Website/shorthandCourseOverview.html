COURSE OVERVIEW

Stenographer may refer to: A person whose job is to write down what somebody speaks through a quick system of signs or scripts and then to transcribe/type the same. A stenographer is a person who is skilled in Shorthand and writes and types quickly at the speed required by the Officer. Shorthand is a subject whereas stenography is the art or the process of writing in Shorthand. It is the combination of words pronounced in English and are written in Shorthand. By learning Shorthand your vocabulary and grip on English Language will improve and it will also fetch you a good professional employment. Shorthand notes are typically temporary intended either for immediate use or for later typing. The main objectives of the Shorthand are to take dictation: to record the minutes during the meeting and later on to transcribe the same on computer
