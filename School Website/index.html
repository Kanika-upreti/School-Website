Welcome to Department of Skill Education

Department of Skill Education has been established with a primary objective to enhance skills of youth of India to meet the growing demand of modern industrialization in India. We realized that while Skill Development was required in our country to ensure that the population was productively deployed, its perception in the eyes of the people has been continuously deteriorating due to the kind of low paying jobs that people got, exploitation and the limited career growth opportunities. While this scenario was true a few years back today Skill Development Trained students are amongst the most sought after due to the requirement of fresh hands in the industry.
