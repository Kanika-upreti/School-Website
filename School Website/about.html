About

Our school GMSSS MHc is one of top renowned school among Govt schools of Chandigarh. This school also offers Skill based courses at Secondary and Sr. secondary levels. Skill-based learning is an approach to education that emphasises developing practical skills and competencies that apply to real-world situations rather than just acquiring knowledge. Skill-based learning is a pedagogy that aims to build the skills of the student who has acquired knowledge through classroom lectures and encourage them to try out those concepts to strengthen the learning process.
